How to Play

  1. Pick your quiz topic above (JavaScript, CSS, or HTML).
  2. Each stage starts with a **Three-Card Monte** game — find the Queen of Hearts.
  3. Correct guess in Monte = +2 points.
  4. After Monte, you’ll answer a quiz question from your chosen topic.
  5. Correct quiz answer = +2 points.
  6. There are 5 stages — score 12 or more points to win the game.
