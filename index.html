Welcome to Code Card Challenge!

Objective of the Game

Complete 5 stages of gameplay by mastering the Monte card shuffle and answering coding quiz questions. Earn 12 or more points to win by correctly identifying winning cards and answering coding questions in your chosen topic (HTML, CSS, or JavaScript). Maintain your streak to increase shuffle speed and challenge!

  1. Choose Your Quiz Topic: Pick HTML, CSS, or JavaScript at the start.
  2. Monte Card Shuffle: Watch the cards shuffle and select the winning card. Correct guess scores 2 points.
  3. Coding Quiz: Answer a multiple-choice question based on your topic. Correct answer scores 2 points.
  4. Progress Through 5 Stages: Each stage has one Monte round plus one quiz.
  5. Win the Game: Score at least 12 points after 5 stages to win!
